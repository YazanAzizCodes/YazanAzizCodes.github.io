After five years of building data systems for some of the most complex defense programs in the world, I figured it was time to start writing about it.

This blog will cover topics at the intersection of data engineering, analytics, and operational decision-making — with a focus on the kinds of problems that don't have clean textbook answers.

## What to Expect

**Analytics & Modeling** — Practical approaches to predictive modeling, demand forecasting, and optimization in high-stakes environments.

**Engineering Leadership** — Lessons from leading technical teams and building analytical infrastructure from the ground up.

**Tools & Craft** — Deep dives into Python, SQL, Tableau, and the tools that make large-scale data work tractable.

Thanks for stopping by.

— Yazan
